Submitted by: Cinos, Rankles

Sonic's running animation is the biggest change between the Beta and the final. Sonic originally had a twirl to show that he was moving very fast, but was later changed back to how Sonic looked back in Sonic 1.

Submitted by: Cinos, Rankles

Sonic's skidding animation was originally a lot longer than the final, with an extension from the Sonic 1 skidding sprite [frame 3 & 4]. The final was shorter, and was fitted with skid smoke.

Submitted by: Cinos, Rankles

Sonic's walking animation was a lot longer as well, with some more fluid animation, but they decided to change it.

Submitted by: Cinos, Rankles

The spindash was kept the same for each, but the Beta didn' have smoke coming out from underneath.

Submitted by: Cinos, Rankles

The Beta wobble animation is actually taken from Sonic 1, just with the new palette. in the final, Sonic has 3 dfferent styles of wobble animations.

Submitted by: Simon Wai

Tails' jumping animation was slightly changed in the final. The original has the spikes of fur around Tails shorter, with the shine missing. The final has longer fur.
